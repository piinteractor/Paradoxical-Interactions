</div></div>

<div class="et_pb_divider_0 et_pb_divider et_pb_space et_pb_divider_position_center et_pb_module"><div class="et_pb_divider_internal"></div></div>

<div class="et_pb_text_1 et_pb_text et_pb_bg_layout_light et_pb_module et_flex_module"><div class="et_pb_text_inner"><h2 class="text-text-100 mt-3 -mb-1 text-[1.125rem] font-bold">The Brutal Math</h2>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]">Nuremberg: 24 defendants. Manageable.</p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]">Subsequent trials: 185 defendants across 12 cases. Still manageable.</p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]">German denazification tribunals: 950,000 cases reviewed. System overwhelmed.</p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>The pattern:</strong> Justice scales inversely to perpetrator count.</p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]">Not because morality scales. Because operational capacity does.</p>
</div></div>

<div class="et_pb_divider_1 et_pb_divider et_pb_space et_pb_divider_position_center et_pb_module"><div class="et_pb_divider_internal"></div></div>

<div class="et_pb_text_2 et_pb_text et_pb_bg_layout_light et_pb_module et_flex_module"><div class="et_pb_text_inner"><h2 class="text-text-100 mt-3 -mb-1 text-[1.125rem] font-bold">The Structural Problem</h2>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]">Post-1945 Germany faced a numbers problem disguised as a moral problem.</p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>Estimated Nazi Party membership:</strong> 8.5 million (1945)<br /><strong>SA/SS members:</strong> ~3 million<br /><strong>Wehrmacht officers complicit in war crimes:</strong> Hundreds of thousands<br /><strong>Civil administrators involved in crimes:</strong> Tens of thousands<br /><strong>Judges who applied racial laws:</strong> Thousands<br /><strong>Doctors who participated in euthanasia:</strong> Hundreds</p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>Total potential defendants:</strong> Millions.</p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>Court capacity:</strong> Dozens per year.</p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>Simple division:</strong> Impossible.</p>
</div></div>

<div class="et_pb_divider_2 et_pb_divider et_pb_space et_pb_divider_position_center et_pb_module"><div class="et_pb_divider_internal"></div></div>

<div class="et_pb_text_3 et_pb_text et_pb_bg_layout_light et_pb_module et_flex_module"><div class="et_pb_text_inner"><h2 class="text-text-100 mt-3 -mb-1 text-[1.125rem] font-bold">Why the Top?</h2>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]">The Allies prosecuted leadership not primarily because guilt was concentrated there (though it was), but because:</p>
<ol class="[li_&amp;]:mb-0 [li_&amp;]:mt-1 [li_&amp;]:gap-1 [&amp;:not(:last-child)_ul]:pb-1 [&amp;:not(:last-child)_ol]:pb-1 list-decimal flex flex-col gap-1 pl-8 mb-3">
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2"><strong>Symbolic efficiency:</strong> 24 names represent the entire structure</li>
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2"><strong>Documentary trail:</strong> Leadership leaves records</li>
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2"><strong>International consensus:</strong> Göring, Speer, etc. = undeniable</li>
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2"><strong>Media clarity:</strong> Public can follow a dozen faces, not a million</li>
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2"><strong>Operational feasibility:</strong> You can actually <em>try</em> 24 people</li>
</ol>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>Justice followed visibility and feasibility.</strong></p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]">Not moral weight. Structural capacity.</p>
</div></div>

<div class="et_pb_divider_3 et_pb_divider et_pb_space et_pb_divider_position_center et_pb_module"><div class="et_pb_divider_internal"></div></div>

<div class="et_pb_text_4 et_pb_text et_pb_bg_layout_light et_pb_module et_flex_module"><div class="et_pb_text_inner"><h2 class="text-text-100 mt-3 -mb-1 text-[1.125rem] font-bold">The Middle Layer: Where Justice Failed</h2>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]">Mid-level functionaries—the bureaucrats, camp administrators, deportation organizers—were structurally the most dangerous.</p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]">They made the system <em>operational</em>.</p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]">But they were too numerous to prosecute and too essential to remove entirely.</p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>Example:</strong> Ministry of Justice under Nazis: 13,000 judges and prosecutors.</p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]">Post-1945 Federal Republic: Most stayed. Because you can't rebuild a legal system from zero while simultaneously prosecuting your entire judiciary.</p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>The choice:</strong></p>
<ul class="[li_&amp;]:mb-0 [li_&amp;]:mt-1 [li_&amp;]:gap-1 [&amp;:not(:last-child)_ul]:pb-1 [&amp;:not(:last-child)_ol]:pb-1 list-disc flex flex-col gap-1 pl-8 mb-3">
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2">Remove all compromised judges → no functioning courts</li>
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2">Keep compromised judges → justice tainted at its source</li>
</ul>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>Outcome:</strong> Keep them. Because the system needs courts more than it needs purity.</p>
</div></div>

<div class="et_pb_divider_4 et_pb_divider et_pb_space et_pb_divider_position_center et_pb_module"><div class="et_pb_divider_internal"></div></div>

<div class="et_pb_text_5 et_pb_text et_pb_bg_layout_light et_pb_module et_flex_module"><div class="et_pb_text_inner"><h2 class="text-text-100 mt-3 -mb-1 text-[1.125rem] font-bold">The Waiting Strategy</h2>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]">Here's the cynical truth: Time solved what justice couldn't.</p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>1950s:</strong> Nazi trials largely stopped. System overwhelmed, Cold War priorities shifted.</p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>1960s:</strong> Trials resumed. Why?</p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>Because enough perpetrators had died.</strong></p>
<ul class="[li_&amp;]:mb-0 [li_&amp;]:mt-1 [li_&amp;]:gap-1 [&amp;:not(:last-child)_ul]:pb-1 [&amp;:not(:last-child)_ol]:pb-1 list-disc flex flex-col gap-1 pl-8 mb-3">
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2">Natural attrition reduced defendant pool</li>
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2">Remaining cases became manageable</li>
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2">Courts could now process what was structurally impossible in 1945</li>
</ul>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>The structure:</strong> Justice became possible not through moral evolution, but through demographic reduction.</p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>Germany didn't wait for forgiveness. It waited for funerals.</strong></p>
</div></div>

<div class="et_pb_divider_5 et_pb_divider et_pb_space et_pb_divider_position_center et_pb_module"><div class="et_pb_divider_internal"></div></div>

<div class="et_pb_text_6 et_pb_text et_pb_bg_layout_light et_pb_module et_flex_module"><div class="et_pb_text_inner"><h2 class="text-text-100 mt-3 -mb-1 text-[1.125rem] font-bold">The Numbers</h2>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>Frankfurt Auschwitz Trials (1963-65):</strong></p>
<ul class="[li_&amp;]:mb-0 [li_&amp;]:mt-1 [li_&amp;]:gap-1 [&amp;:not(:last-child)_ul]:pb-1 [&amp;:not(:last-child)_ol]:pb-1 list-disc flex flex-col gap-1 pl-8 mb-3">
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2">22 defendants</li>
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2">6 years of preparation</li>
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2">20 months of proceedings</li>
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2">360 witnesses</li>
</ul>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>One trial.</strong> Enormous effort. Limited scope.</p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]">Now multiply that by the tens of thousands of potential defendants.</p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>Structural impossibility</strong> becomes visible.</p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]">The system couldn't process them. Not wouldn't. Couldn't.</p>
</div></div>

<div class="et_pb_divider_6 et_pb_divider et_pb_space et_pb_divider_position_center et_pb_module"><div class="et_pb_divider_internal"></div></div>

<div class="et_pb_text_7 et_pb_text et_pb_bg_layout_light et_pb_module et_flex_module"><div class="et_pb_text_inner"><h2 class="text-text-100 mt-3 -mb-1 text-[1.125rem] font-bold">Entnazification: The Mass Processing Failure</h2>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]">Allied attempt at mass justice:</p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>Process:</strong></p>
<ul class="[li_&amp;]:mb-0 [li_&amp;]:mt-1 [li_&amp;]:gap-1 [&amp;:not(:last-child)_ul]:pb-1 [&amp;:not(:last-child)_ol]:pb-1 list-disc flex flex-col gap-1 pl-8 mb-3">
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2">13.4 million questionnaires issued (Western zones)</li>
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2">3.7 million cases processed</li>
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2">950,000 formal hearings</li>
</ul>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>Results:</strong></p>
<ul class="[li_&amp;]:mb-0 [li_&amp;]:mt-1 [li_&amp;]:gap-1 [&amp;:not(:last-child)_ul]:pb-1 [&amp;:not(:last-child)_ol]:pb-1 list-disc flex flex-col gap-1 pl-8 mb-3">
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2">Overwhelmed tribunals</li>
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2">Perfunctory reviews (15 minutes per case average)</li>
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2">"Persilscheine" (whitewash certificates) exchanged among friends</li>
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2">Mass amnesties to clear backlog</li>
</ul>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>1951:</strong> Most proceedings ended. Not because justice was satisfied. Because the system couldn't continue.</p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>Classic PI:</strong> The attempt to do justice at scale destroyed the possibility of justice.</p>
</div></div>

<div class="et_pb_divider_7 et_pb_divider et_pb_space et_pb_divider_position_center et_pb_module"><div class="et_pb_divider_internal"></div></div>

<div class="et_pb_text_8 et_pb_text et_pb_bg_layout_light et_pb_module et_flex_module"><div class="et_pb_text_inner"><h2 class="text-text-100 mt-3 -mb-1 text-[1.125rem] font-bold">Rwanda: Same Structure, Compressed Timeline</h2>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]">Post-genocide Rwanda (1994):</p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>Estimated perpetrators:</strong> 100,000+<br /><strong>Court capacity:</strong> Dozens per year<br /><strong>Time to process at normal speed:</strong> Centuries</p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>The math didn't work.</strong></p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>Solution attempts:</strong></p>
<ol class="[li_&amp;]:mb-0 [li_&amp;]:mt-1 [li_&amp;]:gap-1 [&amp;:not(:last-child)_ul]:pb-1 [&amp;:not(:last-child)_ol]:pb-1 list-decimal flex flex-col gap-1 pl-8 mb-3">
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2"><strong>Regular courts:</strong> Overwhelmed immediately</li>
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2"><strong>Gacaca courts:</strong> Community tribunals, lower standards</li>
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2"><strong>Mass releases:</strong> Eventually, prison population unsustainable</li>
</ol>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>Same structure as Germany:</strong> Perpetrator count exceeded system capacity.</p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>Different timeline:</strong> Compressed into years, not decades.</p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>Same result:</strong> Justice became operationally impossible at scale demanded.</p>
</div></div>

<div class="et_pb_divider_8 et_pb_divider et_pb_space et_pb_divider_position_center et_pb_module"><div class="et_pb_divider_internal"></div></div>

<div class="et_pb_text_9 et_pb_text et_pb_bg_layout_light et_pb_module et_flex_module"><div class="et_pb_text_inner"><h2 class="text-text-100 mt-3 -mb-1 text-[1.125rem] font-bold">The Capacity Constraint</h2>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]">Justice systems have throughput limits:</p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>Variables:</strong></p>
<ul class="[li_&amp;]:mb-0 [li_&amp;]:mt-1 [li_&amp;]:gap-1 [&amp;:not(:last-child)_ul]:pb-1 [&amp;:not(:last-child)_ol]:pb-1 list-disc flex flex-col gap-1 pl-8 mb-3">
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2">Judges available</li>
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2">Courts available</li>
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2">Evidence processing capacity</li>
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2">Witness availability</li>
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2">Defense resources</li>
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2">Time per trial</li>
</ul>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>When crime is systemic, these limits collide with moral necessity.</strong></p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>Example calculation (simplified):</strong></p>
<ul class="[li_&amp;]:mb-0 [li_&amp;]:mt-1 [li_&amp;]:gap-1 [&amp;:not(:last-child)_ul]:pb-1 [&amp;:not(:last-child)_ol]:pb-1 list-disc flex flex-col gap-1 pl-8 mb-3">
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2">10,000 defendants</li>
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2">Average trial: 6 months</li>
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2">10 courts available</li>
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2">Sequential processing</li>
</ul>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>Time required:</strong> 500 years.</p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>System response:</strong> Don't process. Can't process.</p>
</div></div>

<div class="et_pb_divider_9 et_pb_divider et_pb_space et_pb_divider_position_center et_pb_module"><div class="et_pb_divider_internal"></div></div>

<div class="et_pb_text_10 et_pb_text et_pb_bg_layout_light et_pb_module et_flex_module"><div class="et_pb_text_inner"><h2 class="text-text-100 mt-3 -mb-1 text-[1.125rem] font-bold">Why Selective Prosecution Follows Structure, Not Morality</h2>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>Who gets prosecuted:</strong></p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]">✓ High visibility (symbolic value)<br />✓ Clear documentation (evidentiary ease)<br />✓ International attention (political pressure)<br />✓ No operational necessity (dispensable)</p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>Who doesn't:</strong></p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]">✗ Too numerous (capacity problem)<br />✗ Operationally essential (system survival)<br />✗ Documentation gaps (evidentiary difficulty)<br />✗ Lower visibility (symbolic inefficiency)</p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>The pattern:</strong> Justice optimization follows resource constraints, not guilt distribution.</p>
</div></div>

<div class="et_pb_divider_10 et_pb_divider et_pb_space et_pb_divider_position_center et_pb_module"><div class="et_pb_divider_internal"></div></div>

<div class="et_pb_text_11 et_pb_text et_pb_bg_layout_light et_pb_module et_flex_module"><div class="et_pb_text_inner"><h2 class="text-text-100 mt-3 -mb-1 text-[1.125rem] font-bold">The Double-Unbind in Action</h2>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>Attempt comprehensive justice:</strong></p>
<ul class="[li_&amp;]:mb-0 [li_&amp;]:mt-1 [li_&amp;]:gap-1 [&amp;:not(:last-child)_ul]:pb-1 [&amp;:not(:last-child)_ol]:pb-1 list-disc flex flex-col gap-1 pl-8 mb-3">
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2">System overwhelms itself</li>
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2">Quality drops (perfunctory trials)</li>
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2">Legitimacy erodes (due process failures)</li>
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2">Result: No justice delivered effectively</li>
</ul>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>Attempt selective justice:</strong></p>
<ul class="[li_&amp;]:mb-0 [li_&amp;]:mt-1 [li_&amp;]:gap-1 [&amp;:not(:last-child)_ul]:pb-1 [&amp;:not(:last-child)_ol]:pb-1 list-disc flex flex-col gap-1 pl-8 mb-3">
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2">Moral incompleteness</li>
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2">"Victor's justice" accusations</li>
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2">Remainder persists</li>
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2">Result: Justice delivered, but partial</li>
</ul>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>Both fail.</strong> One operationally. One morally.</p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>No third option exists.</strong></p>
</div></div>

<div class="et_pb_divider_11 et_pb_divider et_pb_space et_pb_divider_position_center et_pb_module"><div class="et_pb_divider_internal"></div></div>

<div class="et_pb_text_12 et_pb_text et_pb_bg_layout_light et_pb_module et_flex_module"><div class="et_pb_text_inner"><h2 class="text-text-100 mt-3 -mb-1 text-[1.125rem] font-bold">The Silent Solution: Wait for Death</h2>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]">No official policy ever stated: "We'll wait until they die."</p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]">But structurally, that's what happened.</p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>1945-1955:</strong> Overwhelmed, gave up<br /><strong>1955-1965:</strong> Demographic reduction through natural causes<br /><strong>1965-1975:</strong> Renewed trials, now manageable<br /><strong>1980s onward:</strong> Final prosecutions, mostly symbolic</p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>By 2000:</strong> Most perpetrators dead of natural causes.</p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>The structure "solved" what justice couldn't:</strong> Time reduced the defendant pool to prosecutable numbers.</p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]">Brutal. But structurally accurate.</p>
</div></div>

<div class="et_pb_divider_12 et_pb_divider et_pb_space et_pb_divider_position_center et_pb_module"><div class="et_pb_divider_internal"></div></div>

<div class="et_pb_text_13 et_pb_text et_pb_bg_layout_light et_pb_module et_flex_module"><div class="et_pb_text_inner"><h2 class="text-text-100 mt-3 -mb-1 text-[1.125rem] font-bold">Why This Isn't Unique to Nazis</h2>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]">Every mass atrocity faces this:</p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>Yugoslavia (1990s):</strong></p>
<ul class="[li_&amp;]:mb-0 [li_&amp;]:mt-1 [li_&amp;]:gap-1 [&amp;:not(:last-child)_ul]:pb-1 [&amp;:not(:last-child)_ol]:pb-1 list-disc flex flex-col gap-1 pl-8 mb-3">
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2">ICTY: 161 indictments total</li>
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2">Estimated perpetrators: Tens of thousands</li>
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2">Most never charged</li>
</ul>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>Cambodia (Khmer Rouge):</strong></p>
<ul class="[li_&amp;]:mb-0 [li_&amp;]:mt-1 [li_&amp;]:gap-1 [&amp;:not(:last-child)_ul]:pb-1 [&amp;:not(:last-child)_ol]:pb-1 list-disc flex flex-col gap-1 pl-8 mb-3">
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2">Extraordinary Chambers: 10 indictments</li>
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2">Estimated perpetrators: Thousands</li>
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2">Most died before trial</li>
</ul>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>Chile (Pinochet era):</strong></p>
<ul class="[li_&amp;]:mb-0 [li_&amp;]:mt-1 [li_&amp;]:gap-1 [&amp;:not(:last-child)_ul]:pb-1 [&amp;:not(:last-child)_ol]:pb-1 list-disc flex flex-col gap-1 pl-8 mb-3">
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2">Hundreds of prosecutions</li>
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2">Thousands complicit</li>
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2">Many cases time-barred</li>
</ul>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>Pattern:</strong> Mass crime exceeds system capacity. Every time.</p>
</div></div>

<div class="et_pb_text_14 et_pb_text et_pb_bg_layout_light et_pb_module et_flex_module"><div class="et_pb_text_inner"><h2 class="text-text-100 mt-3 -mb-1 text-[1.125rem] font-bold">The Economics Become Visible</h2>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]">Justice has a <strong>cost structure:</strong></p>
<ul class="[li_&amp;]:mb-0 [li_&amp;]:mt-1 [li_&amp;]:gap-1 [&amp;:not(:last-child)_ul]:pb-1 [&amp;:not(:last-child)_ol]:pb-1 list-disc flex flex-col gap-1 pl-8 mb-3">
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2"><strong>Per case:</strong> Judges, courtrooms, prosecutors, defense, time</li>
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2"><strong>Scaling:</strong> Linear (double cases ≠ half time per case)</li>
<li class="whitespace-normal break-words pl-2"><strong>Quality trade-off:</strong> Speed vs. thoroughness</li>
</ul>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>When defendant count exceeds capacity:</strong></p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>Option A:</strong> Lower standards (faster trials, less rigor)<br /><strong>Option B:</strong> Selective prosecution (fewer cases, maintain standards)<br /><strong>Option C:</strong> Abandon attempt (system gives up)</p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>Germany did all three</strong> at different stages.</p>
</div></div>

<div class="et_pb_divider_14 et_pb_divider et_pb_space et_pb_divider_position_center et_pb_module"><div class="et_pb_divider_internal"></div></div>

<div class="et_pb_text_15 et_pb_text et_pb_bg_layout_light et_pb_module et_flex_module"><div class="et_pb_text_inner"><h2 class="text-text-100 mt-3 -mb-1 text-[1.125rem] font-bold">The PI Structure</h2>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>The more perpetrators there are, the less justice is structurally possible.</strong></p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]">Not because guilt diminishes. Because capacity runs out.</p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>Moral demand:</strong> Prosecute all guilty<br /><strong>System capacity:</strong> Can prosecute dozens, maybe hundreds<br /><strong>Reality:</strong> Millions involved</p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>Result:</strong> Justice scales inversely to crime scale.</p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>The worse the crime, the less accountability possible.</strong></p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]">That's not moral failure. That's structural mathematics.</p>
</div></div>

<div class="et_pb_divider_15 et_pb_divider et_pb_space et_pb_divider_position_center et_pb_module"><div class="et_pb_divider_internal"></div></div>

<div class="et_pb_text_16 et_pb_text et_pb_bg_layout_light et_pb_module et_flex_module"><div class="et_pb_text_inner"><h2 class="text-text-100 mt-3 -mb-1 text-[1.125rem] font-bold">What This Means</h2>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>1. Symbolic trials serve system needs, not moral completion</strong></p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]">Nuremberg wasn't "justice done." It was "justice possible within capacity constraints while signaling moral clarity."</p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>2. Reintegration isn't forgiveness—it's operational necessity</strong></p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]">Germany didn't "forgive" Nazi administrators. It lacked capacity to replace them while prosecuting them.</p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>3. Time becomes justice enabler through attrition</strong></p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]">Not moral evolution. Demographic reduction.</p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>4. Mass crime guarantees incomplete justice</strong></p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]">Not as failure. As structural law.</p>
</div></div>

<div class="et_pb_divider_16 et_pb_divider et_pb_space et_pb_divider_position_center et_pb_module"><div class="et_pb_divider_internal"></div></div>

<div class="et_pb_text_17 et_pb_text et_pb_bg_layout_light et_pb_module et_flex_module"><div class="et_pb_text_inner"><h2 class="text-text-100 mt-3 -mb-1 text-[1.125rem] font-bold">The Cynical Truth</h2>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>You don't need to forgive perpetrators. You just need to outlive them.</strong></p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]">If you can't prosecute millions, wait until natural causes reduce the number to thousands. Then hundreds. Then dozens.</p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>Eventually, justice becomes feasible again.</strong></p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]">Not because society evolved. Because the defendant pool shrank.</p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>This is how systems navigate what they cannot solve.</strong></p>
</div></div>

<div class="et_pb_divider_17 et_pb_divider et_pb_space et_pb_divider_position_center et_pb_module"><div class="et_pb_divider_internal"></div></div>

<div class="et_pb_text_18 et_pb_text et_pb_bg_layout_light et_pb_module et_flex_module"><div class="et_pb_text_inner"><h2 class="text-text-100 mt-3 -mb-1 text-[1.125rem] font-bold">Documentation, Not Judgment</h2>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]">This isn't about whether Germany "should have" done more.</p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]">The structure made "more" operationally impossible.</p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>The question isn't moral:</strong> "Should they have prosecuted everyone?"</p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>The question is structural:</strong> "Could any system have prosecuted millions without collapsing?"</p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>Answer:</strong> No.</p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>That doesn't excuse anyone. It explains the structure.</strong></p>
</div></div>

<div class="et_pb_divider_18 et_pb_divider et_pb_space et_pb_divider_position_center et_pb_module"><div class="et_pb_divider_internal"></div></div>

<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>Nuremberg + Subsequent Trials:</strong> ~5,000 convicted<br /><strong>German courts (1945-2005):</strong> ~16,000 convicted<br /><strong>Estimated perpetrators:</strong> Millions</p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>Conviction rate:</strong> &lt;1%</p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]">Not because 99% were innocent.</p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]">Because the system couldn't process them.</p>
<p class="font-claude-response-body break-words whitespace-normal leading-[1.7]"><strong>Economics of justice:</strong> Capacity always limits accountability in mass crime.</p>
</div></div>
