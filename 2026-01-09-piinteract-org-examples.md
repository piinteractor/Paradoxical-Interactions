# Examples

**PI exist everywhere. Once you see the pattern, you can’t unsee it.**

These are not isolated failures. They are structural features of how rational actors interact when their goals, constraints, and perspectives collide.

Each example shows the same dynamic: everyone acts reasonably from their position. The collective outcome is chaos.

### Systems & Governance

Democracy eroding itself. Climate summits producing emissions. Legal processes undermining legality.

### Technology & AI

Safety measures creating vulnerabilities. Competition preventing cooperation. Optimization slowing systems down.

### Organizations

Departments succeeding locally while the whole fails. Change initiatives breeding resistance. Best practices blocking adaptation.

### Society & Culture

Four buses in a roundabout. Emergency lanes blocking ambulances. Rational decisions creating collective gridlock.

### Science & Academia

Skepticism becoming dogma. Quality control preventing innovation. Peer review as gatekeeping.

### Religion & Belief Systems

Where truth becomes heresy, reformers become inquisitors, and institutions crucify what they worship.

### War & Conflict

Wars begin for reasons. Continue for structure. End from depletion. The peacemaker becomes the traitor. The cycle repeats.
